About Me

Why Lob: What sets Lob apart from other internship opportunities for me is the focus on bridging work in the Software Engineering field to the world outside of technology.

I chose the Software Engineering field because I found it to be the intersection of my academic strengths in both creative and technical areas. However, I have never planned on using my Computer Science degree to earn the highest salary possible at a large corporation. I chose Software Engineering because I am good at it, and in order to maximize the impact I can have on the issues I care about I will apply my personal strengths to the best of my ability.
